Refund Policy

1. 72-Hour Money-Back Guarantee

AxionHost offers a 72-hour money-back guarantee on all new hosting service purchases. If you are unsatisfied with the performance or service quality within the first 72 hours of activation, you are eligible for a full refund, provided the service has not been heavily utilized or abused.

2. Exclusions from Refund Policy

Refunds are NOT granted for: (a) Services cancelled by AxionHost due to Terms of Service violations; (b) Setup fees, domain registrations, or third-party licenses; (c) Renewals of existing services (all renewals are final).

3. Requesting a Refund

To initiate a refund, you must submit a support ticket via the Client Area clearly stating the reason for cancellation within the 72-hour window. Refunds are processed within 7-14 business days after verification.

4. Resource Usage

If your server shows excessive CPU usage, disk I/O saturation, or bandwidth abuse during the trial period, the refund eligibility may be revoked at our sole discretion.

This policy is designed to protect both our clients and our infrastructure integrity.
